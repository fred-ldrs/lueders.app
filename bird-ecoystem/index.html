🐦 The Bird Ecosystem – Apps That Belong to You

Introduction

The Bird Ecosystem is a collection of small, focused apps — each designed to solve one clearly defined problem.

Every app is represented by its own bird, symbolizing its character and purpose. Together, they form a lightweight, private, and expandable system that works without cloud services, accounts, or distractions.

The Birds and Their Meaning

  • 🕊️ SouvenirBird – The Dove
    The bird of memories.
    SouvenirBird captures moments, thoughts, and small everyday highlights.
    The dove represents messages and memories that are preserved and carried forward.
  • 🐦 DadBird – The Blue Tit (in development)
    The bird of everyday life.
    DadBird helps with the small, practical tasks of family life: tracking sizes, simple tools, sleep sounds.
    The blue tit stands for closeness, care, and pragmatic solutions.
  • 🐦‍⬛ ItemBird – The Magpie (in development)
    The bird of things.
    ItemBird manages objects, inventory, and belongings.
    The magpie symbolizes collecting, organizing, and rediscovering items.

Philosophy of the Bird Ecosystem

  • Minimalistic
    Each app does only what is truly necessary.
  • Modular
    Every app works independently — but they complement each other perfectly.
  • Private
    No cloud, no accounts, no ads.
  • Expandable
    New modules can be added at any time without overloading the system.
