# Privacy Policy – DadBird (Android)

**Responsible Party**  
Frederik Lüders  
lueders.app  
Email: hello@lueders.app

---

## 1. General Information
DadBird is a minimalist parenting app for dads, designed to work **fully offline**.  
All data is stored **exclusively on your device**.

There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking technologies.

---

## 2. Data Processed by the App
DadBird processes only the data you voluntarily enter:

- Children's names and birth dates  
- Body measurements (height, weight, head circumference)  
- Fever log entries (temperature, timestamp, optional notes)  

**Not collected:**  
No photos, no location data, no usage profiles, no device identifiers.

All data remains **on your device** and is never transmitted to any server or external service.

---

## 3. Purpose of Data Processing
The data you enter is used solely to:

- Track your children's growth measurements over time  
- Log fever entries and their progression  
- Display this information within the app  

No other processing takes place.

---

## 4. Storage & Security
All data is stored in the **local app storage on your device** (Android SharedPreferences / app-private storage).

The app does **not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or cloud services**.  
The INTERNET permission is declared in the app solely to enable opening external links in the browser (e.g., links to other apps). No user data is ever transmitted over the internet.

**Your responsibility:**  
Securing your device (e.g., screen lock, device encryption) is your responsibility.

---

## 5. Third‑Party Services
The app does not use any analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

The app may launch other apps installed on your device (e.g., SouvenirBird, BeBeBirdie) if you choose to do so. No data is passed to these apps automatically.

**No user data is shared** with any third parties.

---

## 6. Export & Import
You can **export your data** at any time as a **JSON backup file**.  
The export is shared via your device's standard share sheet and remains entirely under your control.

You can also **import** a previously saved JSON backup to restore your data.

The app never transfers data automatically. All exports are initiated by you.

---

## 7. Legal Basis (GDPR)
The legal basis for processing your data is **Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR** – consent by actively using the app and entering personal information.

---

## 8. Your Rights
Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

- **Right of access** (Art. 15 GDPR)  
- **Right to rectification** (Art. 16 GDPR)  
- **Right to erasure** (Art. 17 GDPR)  
- **Right to data portability** (Art. 20 GDPR)  

You can exercise these rights at any time:

- Edit or delete your data directly in the app  
- Export your data using the built‑in backup function  
- Uninstall the app to remove all data from your device
